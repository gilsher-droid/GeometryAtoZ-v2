# Geometry A to Z
## Vision

Geometry A to Z is a Fundamatics learning platform for teaching geometry through construction, investigation, reasoning, claims, and justifications.

The central goal is not only that students know geometric facts, but that they learn to build mathematical claims and justify them.

The platform is based on real teaching sequences and will grow lesson by lesson.

Each lesson should combine:

- mathematical content
- student interaction
- written thinking
- construction
- feedback
- reflection

The platform should preserve the student's work, including answers, claims, justifications, and constructions.

In the future, the platform may include teacher dashboards, AI-supported formative feedback, progress tracking, and reusable learning components for other mathematical subjects.

Geometry A to Z is the first course built on the broader Fundamatics Learning Engine.
